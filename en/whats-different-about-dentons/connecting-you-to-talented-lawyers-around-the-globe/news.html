Dentons advises Applus on the acquisition of Reliable Analysis

October 5, 2020

Global law firm Dentons has advised Applus+, a world-leading company in testing, inspection and certification, on acquisition of Reliable Analysis, a laboratory focused on materials, component, electrical and electromagnetic compatibility testing.  Applus+ has purchased the entire share capital of Reliable Analysis from the company's founders for an initial consideration of €67 million (plus an undisclosed deferred consideration).

Dentons Kensington Swan elects new Chair

October 2, 2020

Dentons Kensington Swan is pleased to announce the election of Hayden Wilson as the firm’s new Chair. Hayden’s appointment takes effect from 1 October. Dentons supports LKE Schneider Leichtbau in protective shielding process

October 2, 2020

Global law firm Dentons is advising the management of LKE Schneider Leichtbau GmbH ("LKE"), a leading German manufacturer of complex transport systems, in the context of the insolvency of its subsidiary in Freiburg-Lehen. The company applied for insolvency in self-administration on September 29, 2020. The Freiburg-Lehen subsidiary, which produces goods for the hygiene and aviation segments, has been impacted by major changes as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, and is therefore suffering financial losses.

Dentons files class action petition with Supreme Court of Hawai`i to secure overdue unemployment benefits

September 30, 2020

Dentons, the world’s largest law firm, filed a petition with the Supreme Court of Hawai`i requesting it compel the State of Hawai`i Department of Labor and Industrial Relations (DLIR) to process claims and pay long-overdue unemployment benefits to the growing population of unemployed residents.
