Stay in touch

Dentons offers many ways to help you stay in touch with our alumni community, from social media to social events throughout the year. You can:

  • Join our LinkedIn Alumni Group, which provides another forum for former colleagues and friends across the globe to network and find the latest news.
  • Sign up for our mailing list to receive emails about the most up-to-date Firm developments.
  • Visit our careers site for employment opportunities around the globe.
  • Check our event listings for gatherings in your community.

We want to hear from you. If you have recently changed positions, moved or want to share your news, please write to us at alumni@dentons.com.
